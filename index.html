about me

I am a Website Developer since 2008, I worked for various companies and sharpended my skills to develop high quality websites.

Most of my development roles depend on the company that I work in. The first year of being a website developer, my role was a Website Implementor, it was more like a junior web developer / intern. Then I got promoted and became a CMS Developer, at that time we were using Adobe Business Catalyst as a platform for making websites.

Now, I am more of a Fullstack web developer, I do most of the front and backend task in creating a website, from converting a web design to a functional website using the latest front end development tools like HTML5, CSS3, jQuery and latest javascript frameworks (nodeJS, reactJS). I still use CMS like WordPress and Adobe Business Catalyst, but most of my projects are in Wordpress since my clients prefer to edit their websites when I finish developing them.

Currently, I am learning nodeJS and reactJS and in the near future, angularJS. I'm focusing more on the front end development of the site since that is where my strengths are. I love tweaking the look and feel of the website using JavaScript and CSS.

Displayed on this website is my personal works and client works (as far as I remember them). Hope you enjoy viewing my personal resume-type website.
